### Project 1: Brahma Accounts [Lite & Pro]

Smart contract wallet built on Gnosis Safe. The platform abstracts infrastructure from account management to capital orchestration and settlement.

**Features:**
- Retail users: secure wallet with strategies, swap, bridge
- Protocol treasury management
- Developer tools for building onchain/hybrid apps without backend infrastructure

**Design deliverables:**
Dashboard components, notifications modal, multi-send interface, automation UI pieces, loader animations for Berachain and Morpho integrations

---

### Project 2: Brahma Imprint — Soulbound NFT

Imprint NFT is a living, soulbound on-chain persona shaped by a user's past and future actions.

**Core traits:**
- **[Arc]** — behavioral patterns
- **[Tribe]** — asset preferences
- **[Level]** — maturity driven by interaction count, account age, transaction volume

---

### Project 3: Swype.fun

DeFi-native spending card that lets you actually use your crypto — either directly or by spending against your on-chain collateral.

Integrates with lending protocols (Aave, Euler, Felix, HypurrFi). Marketing positions product around early crypto ethos and culture.

---

### Project 4: BuyCrypt — Mobile Trading Terminal

Pre-FTX era project (2021–early 2022). Mobile terminal allowing users to connect CEX API keys, track portfolios, trade spot/perp markets, copy-trade strategies, join tournaments, manage accounts.

About

### Bio

I'm Kostya. Some of you might know me as @roark_0x on X. I’m a senior product designer who works closely with crypto founders and DeFi teams. Love building things people genuinely want to try, and return to after that very first touch.

### Constraints

There are things that quietly shape you as a person – I call them constraints. They sit deep in your mind and influence how you communicate, how you choose objectives, and how you make decisions. A framework behind every product you ship.

For me, the core constraint has always been curiosity. I’ve always been explorative by nature, drawn to understand how things work and why people behave the way they do. This evolved into a work style rooted in systematic thinking, responsibility, and experimentation. Think of it as a map, always gets me somewhere interesting.

### What's now

For the past 4 years I've been working at Brahma FI, designing a programmable account app that combines DeFi and execution automation. Recently Brahma has been acquired by Polymarket. Currently exploring a few pet projects, jumping into hackathons with my friends, and writing a PhD thesis in philosophy – a mix that keeps both my analytical and creative sides alive.
